Supermarket sales report analysis

This report offers a comprehensive analysis of sales data obtained from a supermarket, focusing on key factors such as customer demographics, delivery status, sales channels, product categories, and total sales metrics. By examining these variables, the report aims to provide insights into customer behavior and sales performance, which can inform strategic decision-making and drive business growth.
I deduced these recommendations in other to optimize sales efficiency.
Tailor marketing strategies to cater to gender-specific preferences and purchasing behavior.
Enhance online delivery services and user experience to capitalize on the growing trend of e-commerce.
Allocate resources towards high-performing sales channels while optimizing underperforming ones.
Adjust product assortment and promotions to align with customer demand and seasonal variations.
As a result these recommendations and analysis, the company generated 10.20% increase in sales volume in the next quarter.
This analysis was performed using Microsoft Excel.

Fintech Financial Model and Analysis

A Fintech startup called Pay Haven wants to create a platform that makes financial transactions an easy flow for subscribers. They plan to launch by January 2024 and I was asked to create a financial model for their business to help them understand their projected revenues, expenses and profits over the next five years. To start off, i had to gather some information about Pay Haven. Here are some key details: The Business operates a cash basis form of transaction
Dividend payout rate is 25%
Revenues: How much funds will Pay Haven make each year?
Expenses: How much will Pay Haven expend on Operating Expenses and other Expenses for each year?
Profit: How much money will Pay Haven make or lose each, and what will their profit margin be? Once I created the financial model, I presented it to Pay Haven along with further assumptions I made and potential risks or uncertainties. This will helped them make informed decisions about their business and plan for the future.
